Team Mission & Tenets

Mission Statement

PEPA delivers world-class analytics infrastructure, self-service products, and actionable insights that enable Partner Engagement to eliminate root causes of Selling Partner contacts, measure program impact with scientific rigor, and optimize resource allocation across CE and SX initiatives.

Team Tenets

1. Data-Driven Actions and Insights Generation

We deliver actionable insights that drive strategic decision-making across Partner Engagement initiatives. Our focus extends beyond answering "what happened" to determine "what should we do."

2. Automate the Repetitive Focus on the Strategic

We treat manual and recurring analytics work as technical debt. Through systematic automation and self-service solutions, we eliminate redundant tasks and enable stakeholders to access insights independently.

3. Partner Obsession Through Data at Scale

We build scalable products and solutions that accelerate decision velocity across the organization.

4. Data Science and Gen BI Innovation

We apply rigorous analytical methods and generative BI solutions to identify highest-impact opportunities. Our recommendations are grounded in causal measurement, not correlation.

5. Trust Through Transparency

We make data accessible, pipelines visible, and assumptions explicit. Stakeholders understand how we arrive at conclusions and can validate our work.

Mile 4: Big Bet Initiatives

Big Bet Initiatives

High-risk, high-reward product investments that could fundamentally transform how Amazon approaches contact elimination, support operations, and seller experience.

Big Bet #1: PathSense - Seller Journey Friction Analysis

Revolutionary approach to understanding complete seller mission sequences

Problem

Traditional CE approaches focus on isolated defects rather than understanding complete seller mission sequences. Sellers navigate complex multi-step journeys where friction at any point can lead to abandonment and generate support contacts.

Solution

PathSense identifies selling partner experience issues by analyzing complete business mission sequences. Integrates multiple data sources to understand full context and identify precise friction points where partners abandon critical tasks.

Current Status

Completing proof of concept focused on FBA Inventory Performance Index (IPI) improvement journeys in December 2025.

Impact

Produces prioritized list of friction points in seller journeys, enabling GPOs to focus CE initiatives on highest-impact friction points rather than volume-based prioritization.

Big Bet #2: TRACE Prioritization Engine

AI-powered prioritization for maximum ROI impact

Problem

RCOs receive lists of root causes from TRACE but lack systematic guidance on which defects to prioritize. We incentivize volume-based CE rather than impact-based CE, wasting planning cycles on prioritization debates.

Solution

AI-powered prioritization engine that scores each root cause on three dimensions: Seller Impact (downstream GMS), OpEx Impact (variable cost per contact), and Effort to Resolve (engineering complexity score).

Key Features

  • Real-time root cause scoring dashboard
  • "What-if" scenario planning capabilities
  • ROI-optimized ranking with estimated impact

Impact

Shift from 15% uniform CE targets to differentiated, ROI-optimized targets per team. Enable formal benefit accounting in CE goals and reduce planning cycles from weeks to days.

Big Bet #3: Unified Data Mart (Data as a Product)

Scalable data infrastructure for self-service analytics

Problem

PEPA responds to numerous ad-hoc data requests requiring manual data assembly across fragmented systems. This reactive model doesn't scale as OAS expands to additional contact areas in 2026.

Solution

Unified, query-optimized Data Mart consolidating all OAS data sources into single source of truth with standardized schemas, automated refresh cycles, and self-service query interfaces.

Key Features

  • Automated daily refresh with data quality validation
  • Pre-aggregated dimensional models for sub-minute query performance
  • Self-service QuickSight interfaces with row-level security
  • Standardized metric definitions preventing conflicting narratives
  • API layer enabling programmatic access for advanced users

Impact

Reduce ad-hoc data request volume significantly, freeing BIE/BA hours for strategic work. Enable OAS leadership self-service capabilities and eliminate data inconsistency issues.

Big Bet #4: Predictive Launch Risk Scoring (Crystal Ball)

Proactive risk management for feature launches

Problem

Business teams launch features with unpredictable contact impact. We reactively measure post-launch via MoB, but by then damage is done.

Solution

Build pre-launch risk scoring model that predicts contact impact using historical launch patterns (similar features, teams, regions), BRD complexity signals (number of seller-facing changes, policy updates, fee changes), readiness indicators (CM checklist completion, content SLA adherence), and Paragon data (baseline contact rates for impacted Atlas labels). Model outputs Launch Risk Score (1-10) with projected contact volume with confidence intervals.

Key Features

  • Workfront integration (auto-score every change request)
  • Risk-based routing (low risk ‚Üí fast-track, high risk ‚Üí deep review)
  • "Safe to launch" recommendations with mitigation strategies
  • Post-launch validation loop (actual vs predicted)

Impact

Prevent contact spikes. Enable proactive capacity planning. Shift from reactive fire-fighting to proactive risk management.
